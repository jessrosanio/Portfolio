Projects

SAZANI

Honors Capstone Project

As a collaborative honors capstone, my partner and I developed a prototype of a full-functioning virtual mannequin chrome extension alongside an extensive paper. Both deliverables addressed online shopping sizing issues for females ages 18-25.

Office-X Queueing System

SMAD Capstone Project

Designed the user interface for a queuing system allowing students to virtually line up to meet with their professors. This system will make it possible for both professors and students to know how many students are in the queue, ultimately making managing the office hours time more efficient.

Fate of Historic Buildings in Harrisonburg and Rockingham

Interactive Design II Project

Developed a website on the conditions of historic buildings in the Harrisonburg community. All text, images, and data were gathered and handed over from a research class within the Media Arts and Design major that partnered with the students in this class.

Immortal Cinema International Website

Web Design Intern

Worked as an intern over the summer redesigning the layout, colors, and content for Immortal Cinema's website. I was also given the task of creating image thumbnails for video covers to display on the website. The current website can be found at https://www.immortalcinema.com/.

Book Feed App Prototype

User Interaction Design at JMU

Book Feed was created as a platform for social book readers to interact with others online. Prototyping for this app took place on Figma and a variety of user testing was conducted to land on the final user interface.

Blue Ridge Free Clinic

Student Designer in JMU Foundations of Visual Communication Design class

Worked with Blue Ridge Free Clinic to create promotional deliverables including brochures, social media posts, and small merchandise.
